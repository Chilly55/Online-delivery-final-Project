About us

Thank you for using our McDonalds App

Welcome to McDonald's, where our journey began in 1955 with the vision of Ray Kroc and the McDonald brothers, Richard and Maurice, who aimed to create a revolutionary space for delicious, affordable, and convenient meals. From our humble beginnings in Des Plaines, Illinois, McDonald's has become a global icon synonymous with quality, consistency, and community. Over the decades, we've pioneered innovations like the Big Mac and the Drive-Thru, staying ahead of the curve while maintaining our commitment to quality. At McDonald's, we prioritize quality and freshness, sourcing the finest ingredients to craft the signature flavors our customers know and love. Our dedication extends beyond the kitchen; we're committed to sustainability, with practices aimed at reducing our environmental footprint. Engaging with the communities we serve is integral to our identity, as demonstrated through initiatives like the Ronald McDonald House Charities. However, our ultimate focus remains on customer satisfaction; whether you're enjoying a meal in our restaurant, using our Drive-Thru, or utilizing our cutting-edge mobile app, we aim to provide an exceptional dining experience. McDonald's is more than a restaurant; it's a family committed to making a positive impact on the planet and in the lives of those we serve. Join us in celebrating our legacy and being part of a brand that continually evolves to bring joy to millions.
